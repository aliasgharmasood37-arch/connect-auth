# AI Agent Onboarding

Set up your Instagram DM agent in 7 simple steps. Most questions are pick-and-select — just choose what fits.

---

## Step 1 — Business Basics

**1.1 Business name**
> Example: Hydro Customs / Glow Studio / FixIt Repairs

**1.2 Your name (owner or manager)**
> Example: Ali Masood

**1.3 Instagram handle**
> Example: @hydrocustoms

**1.4 What type of business do you run?** *(pick one)*
- [ ] Walk-in store / shop
- [ ] Studio (salon, tattoo, photo, design, etc.)
- [ ] Home visit / we go to the customer
- [ ] Online / virtual service
- [ ] Clinic / office (by appointment)
- [ ] Hybrid (mix of above)

**1.5 Where are you located?**
> Short address + city
> Example: Grant Road, Mumbai

**1.6 Working hours** *(pick one)*
- [ ] Mon–Sat, morning to evening (e.g. 10 AM – 8 PM)
- [ ] Mon–Fri, standard business hours (e.g. 9 AM – 6 PM)
- [ ] All 7 days
- [ ] By appointment only
- [ ] Flexible / varies
- [ ] Other: ___

**1.7 Describe what you do in 1–2 lines**
> Write like you're explaining to a friend who's never heard of your business.
> Example: "We do hydro dipping for helmets, bike tanks, car parts, and full bikes. Custom designs available."
> Example: "Home deep-cleaning for apartments — we come to you with all equipment."

**1.8 What do you NOT offer?** *(things people commonly ask but you don't do)*
> Example: "No vinyl wrapping, painting, or ceramic coating"
> Example: "No permanent tattoos — only henna and temporary art"
> Leave blank if nothing specific.

**1.9 How long have you been in business?** *(pick one)*
- [ ] Less than 1 year
- [ ] 1–2 years
- [ ] 3–5 years
- [ ] 5–10 years
- [ ] 10+ years

**1.10 Any proof points your agent can mention?** *(pick all that apply)*

- [ ] Number of projects / jobs completed (approx): ___
- [ ] Featured in media / press
- [ ] Celebrity / influencer clients
- [ ] Awards or certifications
- [ ] Google / Instagram reviews count: ___
- [ ] None yet — still growing
- [ ] Other: ___

**1.11 Share 3–5 links to Instagram posts or reels that best represent your work**
> These help your AI agent reference real examples when customers ask "show me your work."
> Example:
> 1. https://instagram.com/p/abc123
> 2. https://instagram.com/reel/def456
> 3. https://instagram.com/p/ghi789
> Leave blank if you'd rather not share.
